Connor's Famous Spaghetti

Boy oh boy do you miss Italy? Ever been? I bet you haven't, in this economy? Well this spaghetti might not cure your desire to travel to Italy, in fact it will probably make it worse. This spaghetti is so horrifingly sugary and fake, you'll mourn how far pasta has devolved since being introduced in America.

Can't afford the twenty bucks it takes to go to Olive Garden? I can't either. While this doesn't come with breadsticks so bad but still tempting enough to make you eat seven before your food even gets here, you'll get the whole Olive Garden experience. Except at least with this your aunt's ex-boyfriend isn't the waiter.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Frozen meatballs
  • Angel hair pasta

Instructions

  1. Boil water on high. Add angel hair pasta for 8 minutes.
  2. Microwave your delicious frozen meatballs.
  3. Drain spaghetti into collander.
  4. Add spaghetti back to pot. Pour over sauce and add meatballs.
