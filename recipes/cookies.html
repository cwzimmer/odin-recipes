Connor's Famous Choc Chip Cookies

Do you wish for the good 'ole days? Do you wish for the days before you had crippling depression and anxiety? You bet you do. Come on down and make some cookies, just like mom used to make! You know, before she was disappointed in you.

These melt-in-your-mouth cookies will take you back to roasting chestnuts on the campfire on Christmas Eve! With their delicious chocolate nougaty center, and their crispy-perfection husk, they will make you feel like you're on cloud nine. These cookies are traditional, sugar added, and gluten-full! None of the new wave health fad. Diabetics need not apply, because these will raise your blood sugar higher than Mount Kilimanjaro. With oodles of chocolate chips, these are sure to make you win the neighborhood bake-off.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Nuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl. Stir up well.
  2. Mix wet ingredients and combine with whisk.
  3. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix very well with whisk or with mixer.
  4. Cook in oven at 375 degree Fahrenheit for 20 minutes.
